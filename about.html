About LeadCap

Leadcap is a high-quality manufacturer of Aluminum Roll-On Pilfer-Proof (ROPP) bottle caps to clients in the pharmaceutical, veterinary and agricultural industries. Formerly Filcaps Aluminum Bottle Closures, we are a progressive firm with over twenty (20) years experience in the manufacturing industry. Our Guarantee

  • Conformity with International Regulations
  • Guaranteed Highest Quality
  • No Deterioration / Evaporation / Pilferage

Leadcap's ROPP screw caps are guaranteed by strict quality control and are manufactured with raw materials that conform with major international regulations for materials in contact with food and drugs.

Our aluminium caps serve as sealing devices fitted on glass bottles that can be broken with a simple twist. The seal ensures that contents remain secure and do not deteriorate or evaporate quickly.

Purpose & Future

Leadcap believes in continuously developing and updating its products and services. It is currently undergoing ISO accreditation under Food Safety Management Systems (ISO 2200, 223 and 9001) as part of its vision to venture into exportation, as well as expand to other types of packaging.

Leadcap Corp. is set to be Philippines' premiere ROPP cap manufacturing company, leadning the industry by providing superior quality products at the highest standard.
