Our Products

We are a manufacturer of Aluminum Roll-on Pilfer-proof (R.O.P.P.) closures for clients in the pharmaceutical, agricultural and veterinary sectors. We produce the following sizes for Roll-on Pilfer-proof caps:

  • 18mm cap – for 15 ml standard bottles
  • 20mm cap – for 30 and 60 ml standard bottles
  • 22mm cap – for 120 ml standard bottles
  • 24mm cap – for 250 ml standard bottles
  • 25mm cap – for 60 ml standard wide mouth bottles
  • 28mm cap – for other customized bottles
